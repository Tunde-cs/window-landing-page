About Window Genius AI

Window Genius AI is a U.S.-based, AI-driven platform that simplifies the window replacement process through intelligent automation, real-time quoting, and nationwide contractor matching. Powered by Hoten Group LLC, we blend modern design, cutting-edge software, and trusted service into one seamless solution.

🚀 Our SaaS Platform

We offer a fully-managed lead generation SaaS solution for window companies across the U.S. Through our platform, local businesses can:

  • 💼 Capture and manage leads from their own custom landing pages
  • 📊 Access real-time admin dashboards for tracking quotes, orders, and appointments
  • 🤖 Deploy our intelligent chatbot to convert traffic into customers 24/7
  • 📅 Integrate booking tools like Calendly and payment systems like Stripe
  • 🛠️ Customize workflows for quoting, follow-ups, and service delivery
